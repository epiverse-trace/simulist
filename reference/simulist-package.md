# simulist: Simulate Disease Outbreak Line List and Contacts Data

Tools to simulate realistic raw case data for an epidemic in the form of
line lists and contacts using a branching process. Simulated outbreaks
are parameterised with epidemiological parameters and can have
age-structured populations, age-stratified hospitalisation and death
risk and time-varying case fatality risk.
